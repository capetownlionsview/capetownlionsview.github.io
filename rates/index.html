Lion's View policies

 

Rates are:

  • Inclusive of 14% VAT.
  • Quoted in South African Rand (ZAR), subject to availability and may change without prior notice.
  • Quoted per night for self-catering accommodation.
  • Inclusive of housekeeping Monday – Friday (excluding public holidays).
  • Inclusive of wireless internet connectivity, up to 1 Gig per day.
  • Exclusive of: telephone calls, laundry service, airport transfers, meals, shoots or function fees.

 

Payment & cancellation terms

  • A 50% deposit is payable to secure the booking. If payment hasn't been made in 3 days of booking, your reservation will be cancelled.
  • The remaining 50% is due 1 month before arrival.
  • Cancellation:
    • 1 month prior to arrival no refund, we will charge the full booking;
    • 1 month up to 6 month prior to arrival no refund, we forfeit the 50% deposit payment;
    • 6+ months prior to arrival, we refund the full deposit received minus R1000 handling charges.
  • We recommend you take out insurance against cancellation due to unforeseen circumstances
  • Booking amendments are subject to a R500 handling fee
  • All cancellation and amendment must be made in writing and accepted by us
  • Breakage deposit; we request your credit card details on arrival and authorization
  • All transfers should be made net, without any charges
  • We accept the following credit cards: VISA and MasterCard
  • Rental Contract need to be signed by main member, credit card should be in the name of main member, passport and card should be presented on check-in 
  • By booking your stay you accept our terms and conditions
  • Minimum stay applies and differs per season
