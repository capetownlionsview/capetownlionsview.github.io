SEA MOUNT RATES AND FACILITIES

Seaview Suite
         
 

Seaview suite is the ultimate in luxury.  It is a large 1 bedroom suite (measuring approx. 56 square metres) with its own entrance, and spans out onto a balcony with sea and mountain views and a swimming pool and garden below. It sleeps two and features a large bedroom with extra length king size bed, separate bathroom with a large shower overlooking the sea and bath, couch, coffee table that converts to a dining table and outside furniture. TV is a Smart 40 inch plasma TV.  The kitchen is fully equipped with large fridge, oven and hob, and many other amenities.  Comfortably furnished, with an open plan lounge and dining room that slides open onto a patio overlooking the sea with garden and pool area below.

View Seaview Suite Floor Plan

Sunset Suite
         
       

Sunset suite is a 1 bedroom suite (measuring approx. 60 square metres) with its own private entrance, and spans out onto a balcony with sea and mountain views and a swimming pool and garden below. It sleeps two and features a large bedroom with king size bed, separate bathroom with a large shower, work desk, dining table and sun-loungers. Comfortably furnished, with an open plan lounge and dining room that slides open onto a patio overlooking the sea with garden and pool area below.

View Sunset Suite Floor Plan

Mountview Suite
         
 

Mountview suite is a 1 bedroom suite (measuring approx. 35 square metres) with its own entrance, and spans out onto a small balcony with mountain views (including Lions Head, Table Mountain and the cable car, and the 12 Apostles). It sleeps two and features a spacious bedroom with queen size bed, separate bathroom with a large shower. Comfortably furnished, with an open plan lounge / dining area that slides open onto a balcony overlooking the mountain.

View Mountview Floor Plan
Penthouse Apartment
Seaview and Mountview suites can be combined for a family or group of 4 Adults and 2 kids under 10 years old. These 2 suites share a main entrance and also share a washing machine and ironing board.

View Seaview and Mountview Floor Plan when combined (Penthouse Apartment)

Accommodation Info
All 3 suites have linen supplied. All kitchens are equipped to meet your basic catering needs with a microwave oven, coffee and cappuccino machine, toaster, kettle and fridge. The Seaview suite has a more complete kitchen. There is a Weber gas BBQ on the balcony of all suites. All necessary crockery, cutlery, glasses and utensils are provided.

All 3 suites are available on a daily (minimum 2 days), weekly or monthly basis. Access to private gym on the property is provided free of charge. Pilates and Thai massages are available at a surcharge.

Discounts offered for long stays as follows:

Cancellation Policy
If cancelled up to 60 days before date of arrival, a fixed amount of ZAR 150 will be charged.
If cancelled between 30 and 60 days before date of arrival, then 25% of total booking charged.
If cancelled between 15 days and 30 days before date of arrival, then 50% of booking charged.
If cancelled within 15 days of date of arrival or in case of no show, then 100% of booking charged.

Deposit Policy
50%-100% deposit when booking made
