The Main House


Five double bedrooms – sleeps 10

Lions View Main House on the ground floor comprises five double en-suite bedrooms, a spacious lounge, a dining room and a fully equipped open-plan kitchen. The entrance is via a footbridge that traverses the Japanese koi pond. Furniture and fixtures by top designers such as Philippe Starck line the house adding a fine touch of elegance. The master bedroom, with its raised spa bath behind the bed has wall-to-wall view of the pool and beach beyond. Surrounded by the large patio and beautifully manicured garden is a seductive infinity-edged swimming pool that overlooks Lions Head.

If you wish you could combine the Main House and Penthouse for a spacious seven bedroom villa that sleeps up to 14 guests.

Main House overview

Size: 390 sq. M (4300 sq. feet)

Guests: Up to 10 guests

Bedrooms: 5 Bedrooms: 2 with king size beds, 3 with queen size beds. Three rooms have flat screen TV's.

Bathrooms: 5 bathrooms: All en-suite, one with spa bath & shower, three with showers and 1 with bath and shower.

Amenities: Charlotte Rhys toiletries, gowns and slippers

Kitchen: Open plan fully equipped and fitted: dishwasher, oven, microwave, fridge, freezer, small appliances and Nespresso coffee machine.

Living: Spacious lounge, dining room and terraces.

Outdoors: Large patios surrounding a solar heated rim flow pool, grassed area, Koi pond with patio, BBQ.

 

Electronics: Large screen HD 3D TV, DVD player, iPod docking station, Private phone. Selection of DVD's

Internet: High speed ADSL WiFi Internet connection, 1 gig free per day.

Cooling & heating: Air-conditioning in 2 bedrooms. The other bedrooms and lounge have fans. Underfloor heating is provided throughout the house. Gas fireplace for those chilly winter evenings.

Views: Magnificent views of Lions Head.

Cleaning: Full laundry facilities and house-keeping service Monday to Friday, excl public holidays.

Garage: Use of remote control operated garage.

Security: Intercom access, armed response and alarm.

Position: 5-minute walk to Camps Bay beach - cafes, restaurants, bars and Shops.

Management: The house is locally managed by Camps Bay apartments.
