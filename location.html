SEA MOUNT LOCATION

Sea Mount is ideally located in close proximity to some of Cape Town's favourite tourism attractions. Visit the Cape Town V&A Waterfront and take a ride on the Wheel of Excellence for a bird’s-eye view of the V&A Waterfront and surrounds. Two Oceans Aquarium is one of the top tourist attractions in Cape Town with its underwater passages and the opportunity of shark cage diving or predator feeding. The Robben Island Museum is also only a boat trip away. If you are feeling adventurous be sure to go up in the cableway and experience Cape Town as seen form the top of Table Mountain. For more information about the Cape Town attractions please visit the Cape Town tourism website - www.tourismcapetown.co.za

Directions from Cape Town airport to Sea Mount Studio (2 Rontree Avenue, Camps Bay)
When leaving the airport, take the N2 highway to Cape Town (CBD).  Follow the signs to Cape Town and the Waterfront (the ocean will be on your right hand side.

At the traffic lights leading into the CBD, the Waterfront will be on your right and the Cullinan and Holiday Inn Hotels on your left.  Carry on along this road which is called Buitengracht Str.  Follow the M62 to Camps Bay. Carry on through part of Cape Town CBD.  Drive over Kloofnek and you will then pass Signal Hill and Lions Head on your right and the Table Mountain Cable Car on your left.

Continue down the hill to Camps Bay.  This road changes name and is called Camps Bay Drive when you get into Camps Bay.  On your right hand side you will see the beach. Drive for about 3 kilometres until you reach Rontree Avenue on your left. There is a petrol Station in front of you.  Turn left into Rontree Avenue and follow the road to the right.

Sea Mount Suites is next door to the petrol station.  The first door (closest to the petrol station) is for Sea Mount Suites. To check in, please press button marked "reception".
