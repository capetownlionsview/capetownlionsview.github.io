Welcome


 

Welcome to Lions View

Lions View is a 5-star graded, self-catering villa set in the exclusive neighborhood of Camp's Bay. Recommended by Wallpaper City Guide as one of the best places to stay in Cape Town, Lion’s View has established itself as the ultimate Camps Bay retreat.

Guests can choose between the five bedroom Main House, the two bedroom Penthouse or combine both for a spacious seven bedroom villa.

Camps Bay

Sit back and let the sun, panoramic views and sound of the sea soothe you. Take a five minute stroll down to beautiful palm-fringed Camps Bay beach, or a 10 minute drive into Cape Towns vibrant city centre and its acclaimed V&A Waterfront. At Lion's View you are with easy reach of it all

Design


Design


 

Designed for the style conscious traveler

Lions View was purpose-designed as a guest villa by leading South African architect Greg Wright. The villa is conceptualized around clean, flowing lines, a palette of soothing colours, minimalist furnishings and open airy spaces. It's innovative, modern architecture perfectly complements its magnificent setting at the foot of the Twelve Apostles, overlooking Lions Head and Camps Bay.

 

Setting


Setting


 

Situated in a tranquil tree lined cul-de-sac, Lion's View ensures absolute comfort and privacy. Whether you want to soak up the sun or open a bottle of bubbly by the pool - Lions View is a perfect vacation from the world.
